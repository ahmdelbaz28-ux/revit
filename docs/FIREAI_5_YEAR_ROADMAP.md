# FIREAI 5-YEAR ROADMAP
## Engineering Intelligence Platform Evolution

### VERSION
1.0 - Initial Strategic Roadmap

### DATE
June 10, 2026

### PURPOSE
This document outlines the 5-year strategic evolution plan for the FireAI Engineering Intelligence Platform, detailing phased development from a fire-alarm design tool to a comprehensive engineering intelligence platform supporting global standards, multi-format CAD/BIM integration, and AI-powered automation.

---

## VISION STATEMENT

To establish the FireAI Engineering Intelligence Platform as the global standard for AI-assisted engineering design, enabling safe, compliant, and innovative solutions across multiple disciplines and international jurisdictions.
